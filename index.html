Corpus Hacks

April 23rd, Bay Area

Corpus Hacks is a 12-hour student-run hackathon for female and nonbinary high school and college students in the Bay Area. Participants will receive free swag, prizes and food. We hope to provide participants with an inclusive environment where they can engage in workshops, learn from industry experts, find peers with similar interests in computer science, and develop their coding skills in a stress-free setting.
